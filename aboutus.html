Our Facility

Kathmandu Care is a state-of-the-art aged care facility located in the heart of Kathmandu. Our facility is designed to provide a comfortable and supportive environment for our residents.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of caregivers is committed to ensuring the well-being and happiness of our residents. With years of experience and a passion for elder care, our staff members provide personalized attention to each individual.

Our Mission

At Kathmandu Care, our mission is to enhance the quality of life for seniors by providing compassionate care in a safe and nurturing environment. We strive to promote independence, dignity, and respect for all our residents.
