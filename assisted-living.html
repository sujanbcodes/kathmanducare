Assisted Living Services

Welcome to Kathmandu Care

Kathmandu Care provides compassionate care and support for seniors who need assistance with daily activities in a comfortable and welcoming environment.

What is Assisted Living?

Assisted living is a type of residential care designed for individuals who need assistance with everyday activities such as bathing, dressing, medication management, and meal preparation, but who do not require the intensive medical and nursing care provided in nursing homes.

At Kathmandu Care, we understand that each resident has unique needs and preferences. Our goal is to provide personalized care that promotes independence, dignity, and quality of life for all our residents.

Our Services:

  1. Personal Care: Our trained and compassionate staff members are available 24/7 to assist with activities of daily living, including bathing, grooming, dressing, and toileting.
  2. Medication Management: We ensure that residents take their medications as prescribed, coordinating with healthcare providers and family members to maintain health and wellness.
  3. Nutritious Meals: Our culinary team prepares delicious and nutritious meals, accommodating special dietary needs and preferences.
  4. Housekeeping and Laundry: Residents can enjoy a clean and tidy living space without the hassle of household chores.
  5. Social and Recreational Activities: We offer a variety of engaging activities and outings to promote socialization, physical activity, and mental stimulation.
  6. Transportation Services: We provide transportation for medical appointments, shopping trips, and other outings to help residents stay connected to the community.

Why Choose Kathmandu Care?

  • Experienced Staff: Our dedicated team members are passionate about providing high-quality care and building meaningful relationships with residents and their families.
  • Safe and Secure Environment: Our facility is equipped with safety features and emergency response systems to ensure the well-being of all residents.
  • Comfort and Convenience: Residents enjoy comfortable accommodations and access to amenities that enhance their quality of life.
  • Individualized Care Plans: We work closely with residents and their families to develop personalized care plans that meet their unique needs and preferences.
  • Peace of Mind: Families can have peace of mind knowing that their loved ones are receiving compassionate care in a supportive environment.
